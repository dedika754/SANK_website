PT. Seaweed Anugrah Nusantara Kita (SANK)

Building a Sustainable Future Through Seaweed Innovation

PT. Seaweed Anugrah Nusantara Kita (SANK) is an Indonesian company specializing in seaweed cultivation, seaweed product development, carbon trading, and educational tourism focused on seaweed and coastal ecosystems.

Through innovation, collaboration, and community empowerment, we are committed to supporting food security, economic growth for coastal communities, and environmental sustainability — ensuring that Indonesia becomes one of the world leaders in seaweed-based industries.

Introduction

PT. Seaweed Anugrah Nusantara Kita (SANK) is an Indonesia-based organization specializing in seaweed cultivation and farming practices, seaweed product processing and development, blue carbon trading, and environmental and marine educational tourism focused on seaweed ecosystems. Our goal is to build a sustainable maritime industry that promotes both environmental conservation and the economic prosperity of coastal communities.

By integrating eco-friendly production principles, responsible resource management, and scientific innovation, SANK actively contributes to the advancement of the national blue economy. Through our blue carbon initiatives, we support the preservation of marine ecosystems while reducing global carbon emissions for a greener future.

Company Vision

To become the global pioneer of a sustainable seaweed-based industry from Indonesia by advancing innovation, environmental responsibility, and economic empowerment rooted in the blue carbon ecosystem.

Company Mission
  • Develop sustainable seaweed cultivation based on international standards.
  • Produce innovative and high-value seaweed derivative products.
  • Strengthen the blue carbon trading industry to support climate-positive solutions.
  • Promote educational tourism based on marine biodiversity and ecosystem conservation.
  • Empower coastal communities through job creation, training, and fair economic opportunities.

Company History

The story of PT. Seaweed Anugrah Nusantara Kita (SANK) in transforming Indonesia’s seaweed industry into a globally competitive and sustainable sector.

The Beginning

PT. SANK was founded in 2025 by a team of experts in seaweed science, environmental research, and social entrepreneurship. The company was born from a shared commitment to build a sustainable maritime ecosystem that benefits both nature and coastal economies.

Organic Cultivation

The journey began with organic seaweed farming initiatives in collaboration with coastal communities. This initiative provided a foundation for SANK to develop a sustainable business model supported by the principles of the blue economy and long-term marine stewardship.

Development & Expansion

Over time, SANK expanded its business to include the seaweed processing industry, producing high-value derivative products while pioneering blue carbon trading through scientific-based seaweed carbon sequestration projects to support climate-positive solutions.

Today, PT. SANK stands as an integrated maritime company that continues to grow by prioritizing sustainability, technological innovation, and the empowerment of Indonesia’s coastal communities — contributing to a greener and more prosperous future for generations to come.

Core Business

SANK develops a fully integrated seaweed industry ecosystem — from responsible farming and sustainable production to global market distribution — strengthened by the principles of environmental sustainability, innovation, and the blue economy.

Seaweed Cultivation

SANK manages organic seaweed farming projects across Sumenep, Sulawesi, Bali, NTB, and NTT, cultivating premium species such as Eucheuma cottonii, Sargassum, and Gracilaria. These cultivation programs are designed to improve both the environment and the socioeconomic welfare of coastal communities.

  • Chemical-free and environmentally friendly farming practices
  • Rotational and regenerative seaweed farming to protect marine biodiversity
  • Supports blue carbon sequestration for climate-positive solutions
Modern Seaweed Processing

A sustainable manufacturing facility powered by green technology to produce international-standard seaweed derivative products for domestic and export markets. SANK ensures that every process minimizes carbon emissions and adheres to global quality certifications.

  • Premium products: agar, natural skincare ingredients, & organic bio-fertilizers
  • Certified: Halal, Organic, and ISO 14001 Environmental Management
  • Energy-efficient production utilizing solar-powered systems
Educational Eco-Tourism

An immersive educational tourism experience that provides deeper understanding of seaweed ecosystems, sustainability practices, and community-based conservation — while promoting economic independence for coastal residents.

  • Eco-tour packages and seaweed farm field visits
  • Workshops on seaweed processing, climate awareness & environmental conservation
  • Entrepreneurship and MSME empowerment programs for coastal communities
